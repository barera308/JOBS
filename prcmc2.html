Job Opportunity - Pak Red Crescent Medical College & Teaching Hospital, Dina Nath, Lahore

Applications are invited from suitably qualified and experienced candidates for the following positions:

Positions & Eligibility Criteria:

  • Associate Professor: Ophthalmology, Anatomy, General Medicine, Anaesthesia. FCPS or equivalent as laid down by PMDC/HEC rules; eligibility certificate mandatory.
  • Assistant Professor: Ophthalmology, Anatomy & General Medicine, Anaesthesia. FCPS or equivalent; eligibility certificate mandatory.
  • Senior Registrar: Ophthalmology & General Medicine, Anaesthesia. FCPS or equivalent.
  • Assistant Manager Finance & Accounts: Master's in Commerce/Accounting & Finance with at least 3 years relevant experience.
