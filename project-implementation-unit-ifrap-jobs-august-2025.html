Program Specialist – Irrigation

Discipline: Irrigation / Civil Engineering, Water Resources, Engineering Management, or related

  • Master’s/Bachelor’s (minimum sixteen years of education) in a relevant discipline.
  • Minimum seven (07) years post-qualification professional experience in irrigation & water management projects.
  • Demonstrated experience designing, implementing, and managing community-driven irrigation infrastructure projects.
  • Experience in program management, MIS systems, and community development projects.
  • Experience in Balochistan or similar regions; strong grasp of local socio-cultural dynamics.
  • Proficiency in local languages and effective community engagement is a significant advantage.

Program Specialist – Roads

Discipline: Civil/Transportation/Highway Engineering or related

  • Master’s/Bachelor’s (minimum sixteen years of education) in a relevant discipline.
  • At least seven (07) years post-qualification experience in road infrastructure (planning, design, construction).
  • Experience in bridges, highways, and infrastructure projects.
  • Strong technical expertise in road engineering, standards, materials, and construction techniques.
  • Proven project management skills in planning, implementation, and monitoring.
  • Familiarity with local context and conditions in Balochistan or similar regions is an advantage.

M&E Specialist

Discipline: Project Management, Development Studies, Social Sciences, Business Administration, or related

  • Master’s (sixteen years of education) with minimum seven (07) years post-qualification professional experience.
  • Proven ability to design & implement M&E plans; conduct baseline studies, mid-term reviews, and final evaluations.
  • Strong understanding of the Results Framework.
  • Knowledge of remote monitoring, MIS, and Android-based data collection tools.
  • Conversant with M&E tools & techniques, especially for community development & infrastructure projects.

Environmental Safeguards Specialist

Discipline: Environmental Sciences/Management, Natural Resource Management, Environmental Engineering

  • Master’s degree with minimum seven (07) years post-qualification relevant experience (development sector preferred).
  • Knowledge of social safeguard policies and environmental & social impact assessments (national/international).
  • Experience with donor-funded infrastructure projects.
  • Experience in Balochistan or similar regions; understanding geographic & socio-economic challenges.

Social Safeguards Specialist

Discipline: Sociology, Rural Development, Social Sciences, or related

  • Master’s/Bachelor’s (minimum sixteen years of education) with at least seven (07) years post-qualification experience.
  • Experience in community-driven development projects.
  • Familiarity with international social safeguard policies, particularly for donor-funded projects.
  • Knowledge of frameworks on involuntary resettlement, indigenous peoples’ rights, and social inclusion.

Gender Specialist

Discipline: Gender Studies, Sociology/Social Sciences, Public Policy, or related

  • Experience in community-driven development projects preferred.
