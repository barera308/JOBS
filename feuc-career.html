Lecturer – Machine Learning & Cybersecurity (Full‑time)

We are seeking a passionate lecturer to join our faculty and help shape industry‑ready graduates across Machine Learning and Cybersecurity. This role blends teaching, curriculum design, and collaborative research in a dynamic, international environment.

Location: UAE Type: Full‑time, Faculty Department: Computing Partnership: UCA (UK) Start: ASAP

About the Role

You will teach across undergraduate/graduate modules in Machine Learning (ML), Data Science and Cybersecurity, supervise projects, contribute to assessment design and quality assurance (aligned to UCA frameworks), and collaborate with industry on live briefs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and deliver engaging lectures, labs, and workshops in ML and Cybersecurity.
  • Develop assessments and rubrics; ensure academic integrity and inclusive pedagogy.
  • Supervise student projects and applied research; foster industry links.
  • Contribute to curriculum enhancement and accreditation requirements.
  • Participate in outreach, events, and cross‑faculty initiatives with UCA (UK).

Essential Qualifications

  • Master’s degree (or PhD preferred) in Computer Science, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, or related field.
  • Teaching experience in HE or substantial industry experience with mentoring/training.
  • Proficiency in Python/ML stack (e.g., NumPy, pandas, scikit‑learn, PyTorch/TensorFlow) and core security topics (network/app security, risk, cloud, IAM).
  • Evidence of scholarly/industry output, curriculum design, or certification (e.g., CISSP, CEH, Security+).

Desirable

  • Experience with MLOps, data engineering, or secure AI development.
  • Experience delivering capstone projects with industry partners.
  • Familiarity with UCA academic standards and quality processes.

What We Offer

  • Collegial, international team and clear academic progression pathways.
  • Opportunities for funded research, conferences, and industry collaboration.
  • Competitive package commensurate with experience.
