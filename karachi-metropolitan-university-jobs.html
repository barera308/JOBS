Karachi Metropolitan University (KMU), a Public-Sector university established under Sindh Assembly Act No. LIV of 2023, is situated in Block M, North Nazimabad at the heart of Karachi's Central District.

KMU is a modern, entrepreneurial, and technology-driven institution committed to excellence in education, fostering innovation, and contributing to socio-economic and cultural development.

Karachi Metropolitan University invites applications for the following faculty positions:

  • Professor
  • Associate Professor
  • Assistant Professor
  • Lecturer

Schools / Departments

  • Karachi School of Nursing

Applicant Requirements

  1. Clearly mention the name of the post and specialty.
  2. Submit updated CV and recent passport-size photograph.
  3. Attach attested copies of all relevant documents (degrees, experience certificates, publications, domicile, CNIC).
  4. Provide two current letters of reference.
  5. Include teaching, research, and practice statements (each max. 2 pages).
  6. Complete publication list with citation/impact analysis (excluding self- and cross-citations, noting highest-cited works).
  7. Government/public sector employees must apply through proper channel and attach a No Objection Certificate (NOC).
  8. Incomplete or improperly submitted applications will not be entertained.
  9. All information will be kept confidential.
  10. If applications exceed expectations, a pre-screening test may be conducted.
  11. Only shortlisted candidates will be called for interviews.
  12. KMU reserves the right to cancel any or all applications without assigning any reason.
  13. No TA/DA will be provided for test/interview.
  14. All documents and claims will be verified; submission of false information may result in disqualification or termination.
  15. Qualifications and experience shall meet HEC and relevant accreditation council criteria.
  16. The university reserves the right to implement future amendments to recruitment and service rules as notified.
