THE UNIVERSITY OF AGRICULTURE, PESHAWAR

Applications are invited for the post of lecturer (03) purely on a visiting basis for Fall Semester (2025) in the Department of Human Nutrition, The University of Agriculture, Peshawar.

The Interview will be held on 10th September, 2025 (10 AM) in the Chairperson Office, Department of Human Nutrition, The University of Agriculture, Peshawar. All the candidates must bring their original documents on the interview day.

NO TA/DA will be admissible for this purpose.
