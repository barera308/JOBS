Institute of Public Health, Lahore

Vacant Positions: Visiting Faculty for Paramedical Courses

Applications are invited from suitable candidates as visiting faculty to teach the following subjects for paramedical courses:

Sr. No Subject Class / Courses
1 Microbiology BS Medical Laboratory Technology
2 Pathology BS Medical Laboratory Technology
3 Mathematics / Computer Sciences BS Medical Laboratory Technology / Public Health Technology

Eligibility

M.Sc / Masters (16 years' education) in relevant subject. Candidates with Ph.D., research publications, and teaching experience will be preferred.

Guidelines

  1. Soft copy of application forms may be downloaded from the IPH official website iph.punjab.gov.pk or directly from Paramedics Department of IPH.
  2. Duly filled application form along with attested copies of education/experience certificates must reach this office by 01 September 2025 via courier/registered post only. Personal submission is not accepted.
  3. Only shortlisted candidates will be called for interview; no TA/DA shall be admissible for attending the interview.
  4. Incomplete applications or applications received after the due date will not be entertained.
  5. Visiting faculty will be recruited for one month as a probation period and may be extended for the complete academic session based on satisfactory performance.
  6. Per diem per lecture will be as per IPH rules.
  7. Note: IPH reserves the right to accept or reject any application without assigning any reason.
