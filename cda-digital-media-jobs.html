Digital Media, Communications & Creative roles — Apply online at cda.gov.pk/jobs

Overview

Capital Development Authority (CDA) invites online applications on a merit basis for the following contract positions (initial contract: two years). Suitable candidates possessing the required qualifications, experience and within prescribed age limits are encouraged to apply.

Digital Media Manager — (BPS-17) — 01 Post

Qualification: Master's or 4-year Bachelor's (First Class) in Mass Communication/Digital Media/PR/Marketing/Business Administration or related discipline. Experience: Minimum 5 years in digital media management, communication strategy, project coordination. Age limit: 35 years.

Key skills: Communication, interpersonal, data analysis, digital strategy, project coordination.

Content Strategist — (BPS-16) — 01 Post

Qualification: Master's or 4-year Bachelor's in Mass Communication. Experience: Minimum 5 years in content creation & digital strategy. Age limit: 33 years.

Work with digital teams to develop content plans, editorial calendars, and campaign messaging for CDA platforms.

Digital Communication Officer — (BPS-16) — 04 Posts

Qualification: Master's/4-yr Bachelor's in Mass Communication/IT/Journalism/Public Policy/Intl Relations or related field. Experience: Minimum 1 year relevant experience in social media, public engagement, journalism, or screenwriting. Age limit: 33 years.

Responsible for managing social channels, content posting, engagement and reporting.

Graphic Designer — (BPS-16) — 02 Posts

Qualification: Master's/4-yr Bachelor's in Fine Arts / Media Studies / Graphic Design. Experience: Minimum 3 years with strong emphasis on social media graphics & animation. Age limit: 33 years.

Portfolio required showing social graphics, banners, animation samples.

Audio / Video Editor — (BPS-15) — 02 Posts

Qualification: Master's/4-yr Bachelor's in Film Production / Multimedia Arts / Digital Media. Experience: Minimum 3 years in editing, audio mixing and post-production. Age limit: 33 years.

Software proficiency (e.g., Premiere Pro, Final Cut, DaVinci Resolve, Audition) preferred.

Animator / Visualizer — (BPS-16) — 01 Post

Qualification: Master's/4-yr Bachelor's in Animation/Visual Arts/Multimedia/Graphic Design. Experience: Minimum 3 years in animation & motion graphics. Age limit: 33 years.

Proficiency in After Effects, Adobe Animate, Blender, or equivalent. Portfolio required.

Content Writer / Copywriter / Developer — (BPS-14) — 03 Posts

Qualification: Master's/4-yr Bachelor's in Media Studies / English / Urdu or related discipline. Experience: Minimum 3 years in research, content writing or related projects. Age limit: 30 years.

Strong writing skills (English & Urdu). Samples/portfolio will strengthen the application.

Application Procedure

  1. All applications must be submitted online only at: https://cda.gov.pk/jobs.
  2. Upload scanned & attested copies of educational documents, experience certificates, recent photograph, CNIC and domicile at the time of application.
  3. Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application form for each post.
  4. Government employees must submit NOC from their department at the time of interview (if shortlisted).
  5. Application processing fee: Rs. 2,000/- (non-refundable) per post. The OR code/QR code for online payment will be generated after successful form submission; pay via mobile banking app as instructed.
  6. Last date for submission: 09-10-2025. Incomplete applications will not be entertained.
Important: Only shortlisted candidates will be called for test/interview. No TA/DA will be provided. CDA reserves the right to cancel the advertisement or increase/decrease the number of posts.
Document Reminder: Bring original educational & experience certificates and attested copies at the time of interview.
