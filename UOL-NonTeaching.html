Staff Required

The University of Lahore invites applications from dynamic and results-oriented professionals for non-teaching positions at its Lahore and Sargodha campuses.

Manager Admissions
Location: Sargodha Campus
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business Administration or related field
  • 8–10 years of experience managing admissions
  • Strong background in admission campaigns and stakeholder outreach
  • Organizational skills and experience with application processing
Assistant Manager Branding
Location: Lahore Campus
  • Master's in Marketing, Media, Communications, or related field
  • 6–7 years of experience in branding, communications, or digital marketing
  • Experience with multi-platform branding strategies
  • Skilled in digital tools, social media, and analytics platforms
Accounts Officer
Location: Lahore Campus
  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, Commerce or related field
  • 2–4 years of experience in accounting (preferably education sector)
  • Proficient in accounting software and reporting
  • Attention to detail with ability to prepare financial records
Admin Officer
Location: Lahore Campus
  • Bachelor's in Business Administration, Computer Science or related field
  • 1–2 years of admin experience; fresh graduates can apply
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • Organized in handling documentation, schedules, correspondence
