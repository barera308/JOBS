Punjab Enforcement & Regulatory Authority (PERA) – Career Opportunities

Government of the Punjab

Punjab Enforcement & Regulatory Authority (PERA) has been established through the Punjab Enforcement & Regulation Act, 2024 with the aim to streamline regulatory measures across Punjab for better and more effective enforcement of special laws.

Enforcement Officer (EO) – BS-14

  • Total Posts: 112 (including 36 already advertised)
  • Qualification: Bachelor’s (16 years education) with minimum 2nd division from HEC recognized university
  • Age Limit: 22 – 32 years
  • Quota: All Punjab (merit calculated division-wise)
  • Posting: Anywhere in Punjab (preferably within concerned division)

Physical Standards

For Males: Height: 170 cm, Chest 34–36" (2" expansion), BMI 27, 1 Mile Run in 9 mins

For Females: Height: 160 cm, BMI 27, 1 Mile Run in 14 mins

Eyesight: 6/6 with glasses. Color blind not eligible.

Vacancy Distribution (Division-wise)

  • Bahawalpur Division: Bahawalnagar, Bahawalpur, Rahimyar Khan
  • D.G. Khan Division: DG Khan, Kot Addu, Layyah, Muzaffargarh, Rajanpur, Taunsa
  • Faisalabad Division: Chiniot, Faisalabad, Jhang, Toba Tek Singh
  • Gujranwala Division: Gujranwala, Narowal, Sialkot
  • Gujrat Division: Gujrat, Hafizabad, Mandi Bahauddin, Wazirabad
  • Lahore Division: Kasur, Lahore, Nankana Sahib, Sheikhupura
  • Multan Division: Khanewal, Lodhran, Multan, Vehari
  • Rawalpindi Division: Attock, Chakwal, Jhelum, Murree, Rawalpindi, Talagang
  • Sahiwal Division: Okara, Pakpattan, Sahiwal
  • Sargodha Division: Bhakkar, Khushab, Mianwali, Sargodha

How to Apply

  1. No relaxation in age, education or physical standards allowed (even Govt servants).
  2. Register at Punjab Job Portal.
  3. Provide complete academic info (Matric, Inter, Graduation).
  4. Upload documents: Photo, CNIC (front & back), Domicile, Police Verification (optional), Driving License (if available).
  5. After verification, challan & ID slip will be issued. Fee must be paid via PayZen.
  6. Deadline: 15th October 2025
