Terms and Conditions
  • The positions will be initially for a period of **one year purely on contract basis**.
  • Local/Domicile **male/female candidates from all over Balochistan** are eligible for the posts.
  • Candidates can apply separately for **multiple posts** as per required experience and qualification.
  • Maximum age is **43 years for PPS-1 to PPS-08** posts as per Government of Balochistan age relaxation policy.
  • **No TA/DA** is admissible for test or interview.
  • Candidates already in Government service must apply through the proper channel and attach a **No Objection Certificate (NOC)** from their concerned departments with the application.
Submission of Application

Candidates can apply **online** via the Government of Balochistan Job Portal: https://jobportal.sit.gob.pk/

Alternatively, candidates can **manually submit** their complete applications along with the required documents:

  • CV (only for **PPS-12 & PPS-10** posts)
  • 02 passport size photographs
  • CNIC (Copy of National Identity Card)
  • Local/Domicile Certificate
  • All academic documents

Applications must be submitted at **Room #27, first floor, Block No-14, Civil Secretariat** during office hours. All applications received after the closing date shall stand automatically rejected.

The last date for submission of application is 27th October 2025.
